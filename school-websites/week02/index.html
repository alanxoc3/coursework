Introduction

  • Who am I?
  • Why am I here?
  • Where am I going?

This HTML document does not intend to answer those questions for everyone. In fact those questions will only be answered for me, so that people coming to this About Me page may actually know a thing or two about me. To begin I will share my three favorite quotes, which I believe sum up my ideals:

The heights by great men reached and kept were not attained by sudden flight, but they, while their companions slept were toiling upward in the night. --George Bernard Shaw, Irish Playwright.

Who am I?

You know my name and guessing is a game. I am Alan Morgan from California, Utah, Idaho, Virginia, and Alabama. I have been around the block a couple times. At the time of this web page creation, I am attending Brigham Young University Idaho (BYUi). I majoring in the art of computer science. One could debate that computer science is not an art, but I claim that claim to be invalid! Any time a person creates "something", that person has contributed to the world of art. Speaking of art, I also many hobbies including practicing piano and creating pixel art. Though I am still an amatuer, I aspire to be like this pianist and I own a pixel art blog here. Back to computer science, this statement describes me well:

I love c++. Along with computer programming as my major, it is also my passion. I spend my free time wasting away creating programs from simple games to personal console programs.


Why am I here?

I am here to to be a good person. I am a Christian and strive to live like one. I thrive off of helping other people. If I am feeling down, I try to find something to do for someone. Not only am I here to help others, but I am here to serve my savior.


Where am I going?

By the time you (the client) reads this web page, I may be going home, to work, to school, to the grave, or (embarrassingly) the restroom. Oh, you wanted to know where I am going in life. Through the course of my life, I am always trying to reach my goals. I have hinted at some of these goals in the previous sections about piano and pixel art. What you may not know about me is that I am big into open source programming and I would kill (figuratively) to be a kernel developer for the Linux Operating System.
